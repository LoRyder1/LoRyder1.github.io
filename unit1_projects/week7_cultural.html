Exploring your values:

What are the values that come to mind when I've been the happiest, the proudest, or the most satisfied?

Courage, Freedom, Growth, Meaning, Vibrancy, and Serenity

I think Courage is the value that means the most to me. Maya Angelou said that "Courage is the most important of virtues, because without courage you can't practice any other virtue consistenty. You can practice any virtue erratically, but nothin consistently without courage." Also Ernest Hemingway defined courage as : "grace under pressure". Courage to me is very hard sometimes and to be brave under the most extenuating circumstances is difficult. But it the most difficult and hard things that bring the most reward. The reward I speek of is not worldly things but something more. Having martial courage despite the difficulties is an end in itself.

Values in my Life ( I try to live up to these values): Courage: 4, Freedom: 3, Growth: 4, Meaning: 4, Vibrancy: 3, Serenity: 3.

How do I feel when I think about your values?
